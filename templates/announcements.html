January 18, 2026

New: Favorite Schools Feature

Finding your client's school district just got easier! We've added a Favorite Schools feature that lets you save your most-used school districts for quick access.

How It Works:

  • Click the star icon next to any school district to save it as a favorite
  • Your favorites appear at the top of the dropdown under "Your Favorites"
  • Use the search box to quickly filter through all 67 districts
  • Your favorites are saved to your account and persist across sessions

Perfect for attorneys who regularly work with clients from the same counties - no more scrolling through the full list every time!

Note: You must be logged in to save favorites. Create a free account to use this feature.

35 Additional Georgia School Districts Now Available

We're excited to announce a major expansion of our school calendar database! 35 additional Georgia school districts have been added, bringing our total to 67 school districts with 739 verified holidays spanning 2025-2029.

This means attorneys and parents across Georgia can now automatically import official school break dates directly into their parenting time calculations - no more manual date entry or guessing when Spring Break falls.

Special thanks to Ilana S. Gersowsky, Family Law Resource Center Associate at The Mediation Center of the Coastal Empire, whose request prompted the addition of Savannah-Chatham County Schools.
