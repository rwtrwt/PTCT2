Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

1. Introduction

Parenting Time Pro ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our parenting time calculation services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you register, we collect your username, email address, and password.
  • Parenting Plan Data: Documents you upload for analysis, including custody schedules and parenting plans.
  • Contact Information: Email address and phone number if provided for guest access.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our services.
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, and IP address.
  • Cookies: We use cookies to maintain sessions and improve user experience.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain our parenting time calculation services
  • Process and analyze parenting plans you submit
  • Communicate with you about your account and our services
  • Improve and optimize our services
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

6. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for:

  • Payment Processing: Stripe processes payments securely. We do not store your payment card details.
  • AI Analysis: OpenAI processes document text for parenting plan analysis. Documents are not stored by OpenAI after processing.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Export your data in a portable format

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
